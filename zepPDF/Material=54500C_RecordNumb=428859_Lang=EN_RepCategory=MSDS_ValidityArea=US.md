SECTION 4. FIRST AID MEASURES 
 
General advice 
: Move out of dangerous area. 
Consult a physician. 
Show this safety data sheet to the doctor in attendance. 
Do not leave the victim unattended. 
 
If inhaled 
: Call a physician or poison control centre immediately. 
If unconscious, place in recovery position and seek medical 
advice. 
If symptoms persist, call a physician. 
If not breathing, give artificial respiration. 
 
In case of skin contact 
: Wash off immediately with plenty of water for at least 15 
minutes. 
Wash contaminated clothing before reuse. 
If symptoms persist, call a physician. 
Thoroughly clean shoes before reuse. 
 
In case of eye contact 
: Hold eyelids apart and flush eyes with plenty of water for at 
least 15 minutes. Get medical attention. 
Remove contact lenses. 
 
If swallowed 
: If swallowed, DO NOT induce vomiting unless directed to do 
so by medical personnel. 
Rinse mouth. 
Never give anything by mouth to an unconscious person. 
If symptoms persist, call a physician. 
Keep respiratory tract clear.
